# Multi-Year Plan (PPA)

## Definition
The Multi-Year Plan (PPA) establishes, on a regionalized basis, the guidelines, objectives, and targets of public administration for a four-year period, as defined by Article 165 of the Brazilian Federal Constitution.  
Source: Brazilian Federal Constitution (1988)  
https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#cf1988

It is further regulated by Law No. 4,320/1964, which defines public budgeting and planning instruments.  
Source: Law No. 4,320/1964  
https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#lei4320

## Purpose
To ensure integration between planning and budgeting by organizing government programs and promoting continuity of public policies beyond annual budget cycles.  
Source: Brazilian Federal Constitution (1988)  
https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#cf1988

## Minimum Structural Components

- **Guidelines** — General principles guiding government action  
  (Brazilian Federal Constitution, 1988)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#cf1988

- **Themes** — Priority policy areas of government action  
  (Brazilian Federal Constitution, 1988)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#cf1988

- **Programs** — Sets of coordinated actions aimed at specific results  
  (Law No. 4,320/1964)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#lei4320

- **Objectives** — Expected results within each program  
  (Law No. 4,320/1964)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#lei4320

- **Indicators** — Monitoring instruments to assess progress  
  (IDB, 2020)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#bid2020

- **Targets** — Quantified expected results and deadlines  
  (OECD, 2019)  
  https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#oecd2019
