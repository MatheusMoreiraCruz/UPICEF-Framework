# Multi-Year Plan (PPA)

## Definition
The Multi-Year Plan (PPA) establishes, on a regionalized basis, the guidelines, objectives, and targets of public administration for a four-year period, as defined by Article 165 of the Brazilian Federal Constitution ([CF/1988](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#brazilian-constitution-1988)).  

It is further regulated by Law No. 4,320/1964, which defines public budgeting and planning instruments in Brazil ([Law 4,320/1964](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#law-no-4320-1964)).

## Purpose
To ensure integration between planning and budgeting by organizing government programs and promoting continuity of public policies beyond annual budget cycles, as established in the Brazilian constitutional framework ([CF/1988](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#brazilian-constitution-1988)).

## Minimum Structural Components

- **Guidelines** — General principles guiding government action  
  ([CF/1988](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#brazilian-constitution-1988))

- **Themes** — Priority policy areas of government action  
  ([CF/1988](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#brazilian-constitution-1988))

- **Programs** — Sets of coordinated actions aimed at specific results  
  ([Law 4,320/1964](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#law-no-4320-1964))

- **Objectives** — Expected results within each program  
  ([Law 4,320/1964](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#law-no-4320-1964))

- **Indicators** — Monitoring instruments to assess progress and performance  
  ([IDB, 2020](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#inter-american-development-bank-2020))

- **Targets** — Quantified expected results and associated deadlines  
  ([OECD, 2019](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#oecd-2019))
