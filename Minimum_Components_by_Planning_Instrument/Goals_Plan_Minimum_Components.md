# Goals Plan (GP)

## Definition
The Goals Plan (GP) formalizes the commitments of the elected government by translating political promises into strategic objectives and measurable targets to be delivered during the administration’s term. In some cities, such as São Paulo, this instrument is legally mandated.
  
Source: São Paulo Municipality (2008)  
[https://www.prefeitura.sp.gov.br/cidade/secretarias/upload/planejamento/Emenda_30_Lei_Organica.pdf](https://legislacao.prefeitura.sp.gov.br/leis/lei-0-de-04-de-abril-de-1990)

## Purpose
To ensure that the government program is converted into measurable actions, with clearly defined goals and indicators that allow continuous monitoring, transparency, and accountability throughout the mandate.  

Sources:  
- Inter-American Development Bank (2020): https://publications.iadb.org  
- UN-Habitat (2020): https://unhabitat.org/world-cities-report-2020

## Minimum Structural Components

- **Thematic Axes** — High-level dimensions that organize government priorities into coherent policy areas  
  ([UN-Habitat, 2020](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#unhabitat-2020))

- **Strategic Objectives** — Expected impacts and outcomes associated with each thematic axis  
  ([OECD, 2019](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#oecd-2019))

- **Goals** — Quantified and time-bound commitments to be achieved during the mandate  
  ([São Paulo Municipality, 2008](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#sao-paulo-2008))

- **Initiatives** — Concrete projects and actions directly linked to the defined goals  
  ([UNDP, 2016](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#undp-2016))

- **Indicators** — Metrics used to monitor progress and assess goal achievement  
  ([IDB, 2020](https://github.com/MatheusMoreiraCruz/UPICEF-Framework/blob/main/references/bibliographic-references.md#idb-2020))
