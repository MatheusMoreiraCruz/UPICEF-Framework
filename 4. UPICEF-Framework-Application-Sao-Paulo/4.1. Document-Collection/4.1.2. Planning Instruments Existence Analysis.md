# Planning Instruments Existence Analysis

This document presents the results of the verification of the existence of
urban planning instruments for the City of São Paulo.

This analysis corresponds to **Activity 1 – Document Collection** of the
UPICEF Framework Application Process.

The objective is to identify whether each required planning instrument
is officially available and accessible.

---

## Planning Instruments Existence Table

| Planning Instrument | Document Exists |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Strategic Plan (PE) | Yes |
| Goals Plan (PM) | Yes |
| Multi-Year Plan (PPA) | Yes |
| Budget Guidelines Law (LDO) | Yes |
| Annual Budget Law (LOA) | Yes |

---

## Summary Indicator

| Indicator | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Existence Rate | 100% |

---

## Interpretation

The results indicate that all required planning instruments defined by
the UPICEF framework are officially available for the City of São Paulo.

This confirms the completeness of the documentary corpus used in the
subsequent stages of the framework application.

---

## Notes

- This analysis focuses exclusively on the existence of documents.
- Content quality and structural completeness are assessed in subsequent activities.
