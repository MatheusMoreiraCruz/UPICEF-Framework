# Target Audience and Usage Context

## Target Audience

The UPICEF framework can be applied by:

### Public Managers
- Municipal planning departments
- Strategic and budget planning units
- Monitoring and evaluation teams

### Researchers
- Urban planning researchers
- Public administration scholars
- Digital government researchers

### Policy Analysts
- Government advisory bodies
- Accountability and transparency institutions

---

## Situations for Application

UPICEF should be applied when:

- Evaluating existing planning instruments
- Supporting planning document revisions
- Conducting institutional diagnostics
- Comparing planning instruments across cities
- Performing academic or policy-oriented studies

---

## Application Scenarios

### Scenario 1 – Institutional Diagnosis
Assessment of whether planning instruments are structurally prepared for monitoring and evaluation.

### Scenario 2 – Comparative Analysis
Comparison of structural completeness across municipalities or institutional contexts.

### Scenario 3 – Planning Improvement
Identification of missing components prior to document revision or reformulation.

---

## Purpose of the Application

The purpose of applying UPICEF is to verify whether planning instruments provide the structural
conditions necessary to support:

- Policy implementation
- Monitoring and evaluation
- Transparency and accountability
- Institutional learning
- Long-term planning continuity
